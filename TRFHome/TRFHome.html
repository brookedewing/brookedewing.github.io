The Rhubarb Flipper

Welcome!

If you happen to stumble across this, please note this is my testing ground for HTML and CSS. It is also a bit of a playground to show off some recent projects (as well as our dog of course!)

As for the name; that's a bit of a homage to a wonderful woman who passed away recently. My aunt Nick would always use the word 'flippin' as her form of curse word around us, and it's become a part of my vocabulary that I love. I figured since some of this space will be dedicated to my furniture flips that it was fitting. Also, I can't eat or drink anything with rhubarb without thinking about her and my uncle Steve - their garden is something spectacular and the first place I tried a lot of fun fruits and vegetabels as a kid including, of course, rhubarb!
